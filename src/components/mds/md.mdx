# Wavelet-Based Frequency Flexibility

Comparisons of Gaussian and wavelet primitives for representing complex texture patterns. Results indicate that wavelet primitives effectively overcome the low-frequency bias of Gaussian primitives and demonstrate excellent performance in high-frequency texture regions.
